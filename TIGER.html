TIGER

The tiger(Panthera tigris) is a large cat and a member of the genus Panthera native in Asia. It has a powerful, muscualr body with a large head and paws, a lone tail and orange fur with black, mostly vertical stripes.Tigers inhabits mainly in forests,from coniferous and temperate broadleaf and mixed forests in the Russian far east. The tiger is an apex predator and preys mainly on ungulates, whuch it takes by ambush. it lives a mostly solitary lifes and occupies home ranges, defending these individuals from the same sex. The range of a male tiger overlaps with that of multiple females with whom he mates
