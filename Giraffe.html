GIRAFFE

Giraffes are the tallest mammals on Earth,they belong to the family Giraffidae and are closely related to deer and cattle. They are native to Africa, with their range historally spanning savannas, grasslands, and open woodlands across the continent.

They are herbivores,feeding on leaves, fruits and flowers from trees and shrubs.

Giraffes are knownfor their unique social structures and communication methods, which include moans. grunts and snorts.
