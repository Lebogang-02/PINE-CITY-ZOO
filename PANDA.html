PANDA

Panda is a bear species endemic to China. it characterized by its white coat with black patches around the eyes, ears, legs and shoulders. Its body is rotund; adults individuals weigh 100 to 150 kg and are typically 1.2 to 1.9m long.It can digest startch and is mostly herbivorous with diet consisting almost entirely of bamboo and bamboo shoots.

Panda is a terrestrial animal and primarily spends its life roaming and feeding in the bamboo forests. Panda are generally solitary.Each adult has defined territory and a female is not tolerant of other females in her range .

Pandas communicate through vocalisation and scent marking such as clawing trees and spraying urine .
